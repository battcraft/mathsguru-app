Minimum Kya Hai? (What is Minimum?)

Numbers can be confusing, but thinking about them as 'Bada Rock' and 'Chhota Pebble' makes everything clear. Let's find the smallest of them all!

Bada Rock

landscape

Maximum Value

Think of the biggest number in your group like a giant rock. It's the highest point, the most money, or the hottest temperature. We call this the Maximum.

99
The "Bada" Number

Chhota Pebble

circle

Minimum Value

Now look for the tiny pebble. It's the smallest quantity, the lowest temperature, or the least amount of coins. This is the Minimum.

02
The "Chhota" Number
