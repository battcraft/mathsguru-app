Lines in the City

Dekho apne aas-paas, geometry chhipi hai har jagah! The city is just a giant blueprint. Let's find some math in the wild.

straighten Distance = Constant

Parallel Lines

Yeh tracks kabhi nahi milte, hamesha ek doosre ke saath chalte hain. Just like your two favorite parallel lines. Unke beech ka distance hamesha same rehta hai.

Transversals

Zebra crossing ki parallel lines ko jab ek line diagonally cut karti hai, toh woh transversal ban jaati hai. Look at all those alternate angles forming!

90°

Perpendicular Lines

Buildings sidhi khadi hain kyunki base ke saath exact 90-degree angle banati hain. Ekdum straight, no bending! Isko kehte hain perpendicular intersection.
