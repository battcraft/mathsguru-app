Important Symbols

Learn the language of Math with the Crocodile Trick!

>

Greater than

bada hai

The crocodile always opens its mouth to eat the BIGGER number!

8 > 5
<

Less than

chhota hai

The closed part of the mouth points to the SMALLER number!

3 < 7
=

Equal to

barabar hai

The crocodile is confused because both numbers are exactly the SAME!

6 = 6
