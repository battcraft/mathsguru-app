The 1000 Rule

When rounding to the nearest 1000, we look at the hundreds place. If it's 500 or more, we jump UP. If it's less than 500, we stay DOWN.

Pro Tip

In the kitchen, 1000ml is exactly 1 Litre. So if the recipe says "approx 1L", anything from 900ml to 1100ml works perfectly!
