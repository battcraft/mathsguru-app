Ray (Kiran)

Kya Sun Ray aur Laser Beam dono Rays hain?

lightbulb

Concept Explainer

A "Ray" has a fixed starting point (Origin) but travels infinitely in one direction. Both Sun Rays and Laser Beams share this fundamental geometric property, making them both true Rays.

Sun Ray Origin: Sun
Laser Beam Origin: Device
