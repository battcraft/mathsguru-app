Ray: Naming the Kiran

A Ray (Kiran) is like a laser beam. It starts at one point and shoots off infinitely in one direction. Naming it correctly is crucial.

warning Hamesha arrow side check karo!

The Symbol

To write "Ray AB", we draw a tiny ray symbol over the letters: AB⃗.

The first letter is ALWAYS the starting point (Bindu), and the second letter is a point on the path of the arrow.

A (Start) B (Path)

Comparison: AB⃗ vs BA⃗

Ray AB⃗

A B

Starts at A, goes through B forever.

Ray BA⃗

B A

Starts at B, goes through A forever.
Not the same ray!
