The strict Maths rule

Maths ki duniya mein, ek "line" technically hamesha seedhi (straight) hi hoti hai. Agar woh mud jaye ya bend ho, toh hum usse "curve" bulaate hain. Ek line dono directions mein endless chalti hai, bina mudey!

route

Shortest rasta kaunsa?

Koi bhi 2 points ke beech ka sabse chhota rasta (shortest distance) hamesha ek straight line segment hoti hai. Curve loge toh journey lambi ho jayegi. Seedha jao, time bachao!

Earth par straight line? 🌍

Mazaedaar baat: Kyunki hamari Earth gol (sphere) hai, real world mein ground par draw ki gayi har "straight line" actually mein thodi si curved hoti hai Earth ke surface ke saath! Par copy par, rule ekdam seedha hai.
