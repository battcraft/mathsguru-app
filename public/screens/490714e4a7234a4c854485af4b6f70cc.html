How to Measure

  1. 1

    Zero on Point A

    Place the '0' mark of the ruler exactly on the starting dot (A).

  2. 2

    Align the Edge

    Make sure the ruler's edge runs straight along the line segment.

  3. 3

    Read Point B

    Look at the number on the ruler where the ending dot (B) sits. That's your length!
